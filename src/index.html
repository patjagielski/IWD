Adrianna Kaczmarek

Animatorka postaci związana z polską branżą fimową i reklamową od 2016 roku. Absolwentka wydziału Sztuki Nowych Mediów Polsko-Japońskiej Akademii Technik Komputerowych, od 2018 roku studentka szkoły Animation Mentor. Pracowała przy takich produkcjach jak "Dolina Bogów"(2019), "Gwiazdy" (2017) czy "Ostatnia Rodzina"(2016). Od tego roku związana z warszawską firmą BadiBadi, gdzie tworzy animacje m.in. do serialu animowanego "Latający Miś i Strażnicy Legend". Członkini organizacji międzynarodowej Women in Animation (WIA).

Character animator associated with polish film and commercial industries since 2016. Graduate of the New Media Art department at Polish-Japanese Academy of Information Technology, student of Animation Mentor online school since 2018. Involved in the production of a number of movies, such as Valley of the Gods (2019), Stars (2017) and The Last Family (2016). Currently working in BadiBadi studio, creating animations for various projects, such as the animated series The Flying Bear and the Gang. Member of an interantional organization Women in Animation (WIA).

Ilona Beliatskaya

Ilona Beliatskaya is a travel futurist and researcher in travel technology. Currently Ilona serves EMERGE as a Programme and Content Manager. Her professional focus lies in the digital transformation of service industries, ranging from the use of interactive media in shaping travel experiences, to the applications of automated technologies (artificial intelligence and robotics) in meetings and hospitality. Ilona has a proven track record in teaching experience in various universities in Europe and extensive industry experience in tourism and hospitality.
